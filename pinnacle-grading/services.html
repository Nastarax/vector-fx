Our Services

Six decades of combined expertise delivered through a single, dependable crew.

01 — Foundation of Every Build

House Site Preparation

Before the first board is nailed, the ground beneath it must be right. Pinnacle Grading specializes in complete house site preparation for builders and homeowners across North Carolina.

We evaluate your lot's topography, soil conditions, and drainage characteristics, then execute a precise clearing and grading plan that gives your builder a solid, level, well-drained surface to work from.

  • Lot clearing and tree removal coordination
  • Topsoil stripping and stockpiling
  • Subgrade preparation and compaction
  • Drainage planning and swale installation
02 — Precision Earthwork

Grading

Proper grading is the difference between land that drains beautifully and land that floods every time it rains. Our operators use laser-guided equipment to achieve grade tolerances that exceed building standards.

Whether it's a flat pad for a mountain home, a sloped driveway that sheds water away from the structure, or a final grade that ties into your landscaping, we get the angles right.

  • Rough and finish grading
  • Laser-controlled earthwork
  • Driveway and road grading
  • Erosion control and seeding
03 — Deep Work Done Right

Excavation

From basement digs to utility trenches to pond construction, our excavation team handles projects of all scales with equipment matched precisely to the job at hand.

We know the soils of western North Carolina — the rocky ridgelines, the clay-heavy hollows, the fast-draining sandy benches — and we plan accordingly so excavation stays on schedule and on budget.

  • Full basement and crawl space excavation
  • Utility and drainage trench work
  • Pond and detention basin construction
  • Rock removal and blasting coordination
04 — Compliant & Reliable

Septic Systems

A correctly installed septic system protects your land, your well, and your family for decades. We work closely with NC Environmental Health departments to design and install systems that meet all local regulations.

We handle conventional gravity systems, pump systems, and alternative low-pressure pipe systems for challenging sites where conventional installation isn't feasible.

  • Soil evaluation and perc test coordination
  • Conventional gravity septic installation
  • Alternative and pump system installation
  • Repair and replacement of existing systems
05 — Natural Strength

Boulder Walls

Boulder retaining walls do two jobs: they hold your slope in place and they look like they belong there. Using locally sourced granite and fieldstone, we build walls that become a natural feature of your property.

Our wall installations are engineered for long-term stability with proper base preparation, drainage, and batter — not just stacked rocks that wash out in the first hard rain.

  • Natural granite and fieldstone walls
  • Tiered terrace construction
  • Drainage integration behind walls
  • Walls up to 12 feet in height
06 — From Raw to Ready

Land Development

We take raw acreage through every phase of site preparation — from initial clearing all the way to lot-ready status for homebuilders, subdivisions, and commercial clients.

Our experience with local zoning, utility coordination, and NC stormwater regulations means fewer surprises and a smoother path from permits to project completion.

  • Land clearing and tree removal
  • Subdivision road and driveway construction
  • Stormwater management and BMP installation
  • Utility corridor preparation
