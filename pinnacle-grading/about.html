Our Story

Confidence Built From the Ground Up

Pinnacle Grading Company Incorporated was founded on a simple belief: that the land beneath every home and structure deserves the same care and craftsmanship as the building itself. Over 25 years of work across western North Carolina have only deepened that conviction.

What started as a one-machine operation has grown into a full-service grading and land development company with a modern fleet, an experienced crew, and a reputation built entirely on word-of-mouth referrals from satisfied clients and builders.

We've worked on everything from single-family home sites on difficult mountain terrain to multi-lot subdivision road construction. Every job gets the same attention to detail, because we know that the groundwork we lay today becomes someone's home, business, or farm tomorrow.

How We Work

Our Approach to Every Project

We don't show up and start moving dirt. We start by listening. Every property in North Carolina has its own character — its own slopes, drainage patterns, soil types, and surroundings. Understanding that character before we put the first blade in the ground is how we avoid costly surprises and deliver results that hold up for decades.

  • Site walk and consultation before any proposal
  • Written scope and transparent pricing up front
  • Erosion and sediment controls installed first
  • Daily communication with the homeowner or builder
  • Final walk-through to confirm satisfaction
  • Fully licensed and insured in North Carolina
