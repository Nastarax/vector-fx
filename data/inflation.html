About Inflation Data

The inflation view shows CPI (consumer prices) and PPI (producer prices) across the 8 majors.

High inflation is a concern for a central bank and can motivate rate hikes, which tend to strengthen a currency. Very low inflation can motivate rate cuts to stimulate growth.

Bars and tables use the latest official reported figures. The historical line chart is computed from FRED index data and may lag the latest release for non-US currencies.
