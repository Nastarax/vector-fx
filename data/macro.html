Where to read

Fewer, better inputs. Spend your time on the thinking layer (daily macro notes), keep fast news on tap but don't live in it, and let your calendar + Vector handle the data.

A simple daily routine

The one rule: as a swing trader your enemy is overconsumption, not under-information. If a headline makes you want to touch a position you already sized correctly, that's the feed doing its job of generating noise. Read to build a view for the week, not to justify a click today.
